About Us

A Legacy of Excellence in Education

Siddhartha Secondary School has been a cornerstone of quality education in Biratnagar since its founding. We believe every student holds the potential to achieve extraordinary things when given the right environment, mentorship, and inspiration.

Our dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and holistic approach to learning create an atmosphere where academic rigour meets personal growth — shaping confident, compassionate, and capable individuals.

  • NEB Affiliated Classes 9–12
  • Science & Management Streams
  • Experienced Teaching Staff
  • Digital Classrooms & Labs
  • Sports & Cultural Programs
  • Safe & Inclusive Campus
