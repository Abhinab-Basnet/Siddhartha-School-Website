{# MAIN MOTTO CARD #}

Our Motto

"Excellence · Integrity · Service"

Excellence Integrity Service

Three Words. One Purpose.

Since its founding, Siddhartha Secondary School has stood by three guiding words — Excellence, Integrity, and Service. These are not merely inscribed on our walls; they are lived, breathed, and practised every day by every member of our school family.

Our motto serves as a compass for students navigating the challenges of academic life and beyond. It reminds us of the standard we hold ourselves to and the responsibility we carry towards each other and our community in Sundar-Haraincha, Koshi Province.

Every lesson taught, every examination sat, and every act of kindness shown on this campus is a reflection of these three words. They connect generations of students and alumni in a shared identity — one we are proud to carry forward.

{# THREE PILLARS #}
Breaking It Down

The Three Pillars of Our Motto

01

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in everything we do — in teaching, in learning, and in daily conduct. Excellence is not a destination; it is a habit we build together.

Rigorous NEB-aligned curriculum
High expectations for every student
Continuous improvement in teaching
02

Integrity

We act with honesty, transparency, and moral courage in all circumstances. Integrity builds the trust that holds our school community together.

Honesty in academics and conduct
Accountability for one's actions
Respect for rules and one another
03

Service

We nurture a spirit of giving back — to classmates, to the school, and to the wider community. True education empowers students to contribute meaningfully beyond themselves.

Community outreach programmes
Peer support and mentorship
Leadership through volunteerism

{# HOW WE LIVE IT #}
In Practice

How We Live the Motto Every Day

Morning Assembly

Every school day begins with a reflection on our motto, reminding students of the values they carry into each class.

Peer Mentorship

Senior students mentor juniors — practising service and building a culture of collaborative learning across all grades.

Academic Honesty Policy

Our strict no-cheating policy upholds integrity in every examination, assignment, and classroom interaction.

Excellence Awards

Students who consistently demonstrate all three pillars are recognised annually in our School Motto Honours ceremony.

Community Service Days

Twice each term, students participate in school-organised service drives in the Sundar-Haraincha community.

Character Report Cards

Alongside academic results, students receive a character assessment reflecting how they live Excellence, Integrity, and Service.
