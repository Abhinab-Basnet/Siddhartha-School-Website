What We Do Every Day

To provide every student with a high-quality, inclusive education that builds strong academic foundations, fosters critical thinking, and develops the character needed to lead a purposeful life.

At Siddhartha Secondary School, our mission is more than a statement — it is the daily commitment of every teacher, administrator, and staff member on our campus. We believe that education is the most powerful tool for transforming lives, and we pursue that belief with rigour and compassion in equal measure.

Academic Excellence — Deliver a rigorous NEB-aligned curriculum that prepares students for national examinations and higher education.

Character Development — Nurture honesty, empathy, and responsibility so students grow into ethical citizens.

Inclusive Community — Welcome every student regardless of background and ensure equal access to learning opportunities.

Lifelong Learning — Inspire curiosity and a love for learning that extends far beyond graduation day.

{# Left content #}

Where We Are Headed

To be the most trusted secondary school in Koshi Province — recognised for academic excellence, holistic development, and producing graduates who contribute meaningfully to society.

Our vision is to build an institution that stands as a beacon of learning in eastern Nepal — one where innovation meets tradition, and where every graduate leaves not just with a certificate, but with the confidence, skills, and values to shape a better future.

Global Readiness — Equip students with skills that meet both national and international educational standards.

Regional Leadership — Become the first-choice school for families across Biratnagar and Koshi Province.

Technology Integration — Embrace modern tools and digital learning to prepare students for a technology-driven world.

Sustainable Future — Foster environmental awareness and social responsibility in every student.
