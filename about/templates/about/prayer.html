Morning Prayer

Recited every morning before classes begin

{# Nepali / Original #}
Original — Nepali
हे परमेश्वर, हाम्रो जीवनको प्रकाश, आज हामीलाई बुद्धि र विवेक प्रदान गर्नुस्। हाम्रो मनलाई ज्ञानको पथमा अग्रसर गराउनुस्, र हामीलाई सत्य, न्याय र करुणाको मार्गमा चलाउनुस्। हाम्रा शिक्षकहरूलाई आशिष् दिनुस्, जसले हामीलाई ज्ञानको किरण देखाउँछन्। हाम्रा अभिभावकहरूलाई बल दिनुस्, जसले हामीलाई यहाँसम्म पुर्‍याएका छन्। आजको दिन हामीलाई एकता, सद्भाव र परिश्रममा राख्नुस्। हाम्रो विद्यालयलाई ज्ञान र संस्कारको मन्दिर बनाउनुस्।
{# English translation #}
Translation — English
O Lord, the light of our lives, Grant us wisdom and discernment today. Lead our minds on the path of knowledge, And guide us in the way of truth, justice, and compassion. Bless our teachers, Who show us the rays of enlightenment. Strengthen our parents, Who have brought us to this place. Keep us in unity, harmony, and diligence throughout this day. Make our school a temple of knowledge and good values.

Note: The morning prayer is recited by all students and staff at the beginning of the school day during the assembly. Students are encouraged to reflect on its meaning as they begin their day of learning.

You can replace this text with your actual school prayer once ready.

Evening Prayer

Recited at the close of the school day

{# Nepali / Original #}
Original — Nepali
हे प्रभु, आजको दिन तपाईंको कृपाले बिताएकोमा धन्यवाद। हामीलाई आज जे सिक्न पाइयो, त्यो ज्ञान जीवनमा उपयोग गर्ने शक्ति दिनुस्। हाम्रा गल्तीहरूलाई क्षमा गर्नुस्, र हामीलाई भोलि थप राम्रो बन्ने प्रेरणा दिनुस्। हाम्रो परिवार र साथीभाइलाई सुरक्षित राख्नुस्। रातभर हाम्रो मनमा शान्ति र चित्तमा स्थिरता दिनुस्, ताकि भोलि हामी नयाँ ऊर्जा र उत्साहका साथ फर्कन सकौँ। सबैको भलाइ होस्, सबैमा प्रेम र मैत्री रहोस्।
{# English translation #}
Translation — English
O Lord, thank you for this day spent in your grace. Give us the strength to use the knowledge we gained today throughout our lives. Forgive us our mistakes, And inspire us to be better tomorrow. Keep our families and friends safe. Grant us peace in our hearts and stillness in our minds through the night, So that we may return tomorrow with fresh energy and enthusiasm. May all be well, may love and friendship dwell in all.

Note: The evening prayer is recited by all students and staff before dispersal at the end of the school day. It is a moment to reflect on the day's learnings and express gratitude.

You can replace this text with your actual school prayer once ready.
