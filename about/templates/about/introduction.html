Introduction

About the Institution

Managed by the Nepal Jesuit Society, our institution is committed to providing a transformative education that fosters academic excellence and social responsibility.

Inspired by the life and teachings of St. Ignatius of Loyola, we aim to prepare students to be "men and women for others." Our approach to education is holistic—focusing on the intellectual, spiritual, and emotional growth of every individual.

For decades, we have been a leader in the educational landscape of Nepal, producing graduates who are not only competent professionals but also compassionate citizens ready to lead with integrity.
