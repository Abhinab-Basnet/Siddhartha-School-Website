About Our School

Welcome to Siddhartha Secondary School

Siddhartha Secondary School, established in Biratnagar, Koshi Province, has been one of the most trusted educational institutions in the region for over three decades. We are affiliated with the National Examinations Board (NEB) and offer classes from Grade 9 through Grade 12 under both Science and Management streams.

Our school was founded with a single, enduring belief — that every child deserves access to quality education in an environment that nurtures curiosity, builds character, and inspires excellence. That belief remains at the heart of everything we do today.

With a team of over 60 experienced and dedicated educators, modern classrooms, fully-equipped science laboratories, a rich library, and a vibrant campus life, Siddhartha Secondary School continues to shape the leaders of tomorrow.

What Sets Us Apart

Why Choose Siddhartha?

We offer more than just academics — we offer an environment where every student is seen, supported, and inspired to reach their full potential.

NEB Affiliated Curriculum

Fully affiliated with the National Examinations Board, offering Science and Management streams for Classes 9–12 with a structured, nationally recognised curriculum.

Experienced Faculty

Our 60+ teachers bring years of expertise, passion, and commitment to every classroom. Many hold advanced degrees and have been recognised for their contributions to education.

Modern Facilities

From digital classrooms and well-stocked laboratories to a comprehensive library and sports complex — our facilities are designed to support every dimension of student life.

Safe & Inclusive Environment

A CCTV-monitored, child-safe campus where every student — regardless of background — is welcomed, respected, and empowered to thrive.

Strong Academic Results

With a consistent board pass rate above 95%, our students regularly achieve distinction in NEB examinations and go on to top universities across Nepal and abroad.

Holistic Development

Beyond the classroom, students participate in sports, cultural events, leadership programs, and community service — developing the whole person, not just the student.

Recognised & Affiliated

Siddhartha Secondary School is officially affiliated with the National Examinations Board (NEB) of Nepal and recognised by the Government of Koshi Province.
